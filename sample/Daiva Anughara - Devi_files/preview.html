Bhairava Anugraha

Introduction:

The Existence of Maa Kamakhya

Maa Kmkhy is not merely a Dev of form, but the

pulsating Yoni Tattva, the essence of creation itself.

Her shrine at Nilachal Parvat in Assam holds the only

living Yoni of Shakti worshipped not as an idol, but

as a bleeding womb, alive and potent.

She is Mahmy, Tripur Sundar, and Bhairav in

one. She is the gateway to Tantra where desire is

not denied, but divinized.

During Gupta Navartri, when the world sleeps

unaware, the Devas whisper secrets, and Maa opens

the inner portals to transformation, Siddhis, and soul

liberation. To worship Kamakhya now is to plant seeds

that bloom in silence, protected from worldly storms.

Why Kamakhya Sadhana in Gupta Navaratri?

Viniyoga (Purpose of the Mantra)

1. Kmkhy grants Antar-Mukti She cuts inner blocks and karmic knots.

2. This is the time of inner awakening, hidden from the outer gaze, perfect for

secret sdhana and energy ascension.

3. She is Yoginpriya, and opens the 64 Yogin Chakra within, awakening

Kundalin and Prrabdha Karma.

4. Bhairava becomes still when Kamakhya is invoked meaning, the wild

within finds direction.

Bhairava Anugraha

Om asya r-Kmkhy mantrasya | iva rsih | Gyatr chandah |

Kmkhy devat | r-Kmkhy prtyartham jape viniyogah ||

This mantra of Shri Kamakhya is revealed by Rishi Shiva, in

Gayatri meter, and dedicated to Devi Kamakhya. It is used for

her pleasure and invocation in Japa.

Bhairava Anugraha

Moola Mantra of Kamakhya Devi (With Meaning)

How to Perform this Sadhana During Gupta Navaratri

Om Aim Hreem Kleem Kamakhyai Namah

1. Choose Brahma Muhurta or Midnight (both are powerful for Kamakhya).

Perform this sadhana on Gupta Navratri day.

2. Sit on a red or black Aasan, preferably facing East or North.

3. Light deepa and dhupa, and invoke Maa through Dhyan & Viniyoga.

4. Perform Nyasa, embodying the mantra in your subtle body.

5. Do the 16 Upachara Puja with intention and inner offering (even mentally).

6. Chant the Moola Mantra:

A. 108 or 1008 times daily. With total 6 Mala each Morning and Evening

B. Focus on yoni chakra (Svadhisthana) and feel the rising energy.

7. After japa, sit in stillness, absorb the vibration, and offer your Sankalpa.

8. Offer Prarthana: Maa, awaken my inner Shakti, make me a vessel of your

truth and freedom.

Aim () Saraswati Bija, activates inner wisdom and subtle speech.

Hreem () Mahashakti Bija, the radiant womb of creation, sacred heart-space of Devi.

Kleem () The magnetic bija of love, attraction, and desire unlocking the Kamakhya tattva.

Kamakhyai Namah () Salutations to the Devi who is the granter of all desires and

the mystery of the Yoni Peetha.

Guru Bhava

Bhairava Anugraha

O Sadhaka, when the world sleeps in ignorance, Kamakhya awakens in you as

sacred fire. Her temple is not only in Assam it is in your Yoni ( Here we are

as Ardhanarishvara Guna, so we are not biological male or female, We all

carry both tattva) , your Kundalini, your hidden wish to rise and be whole.

Gupta Navaratri is not about outer show. It is about inner alchemy. And

Kamakhya is the Yogini of Alchemy. Approach her not with fear, but with

surrender, desire, and fire. She responds only to the one ready to dissolve.

Our Guru Bhairava is with us in every breath of this sdhana, in every

silence between the chants. As you walk this sacred path, may His fierce

compassion and boundless grace carry you through all the coming days.

You are not alone He walks with you.

Jai Bhaiarva, Jai Maa Kamakya
